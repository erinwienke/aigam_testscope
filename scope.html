AIGAM Test

Test Scope

Automatic Itinerary Generator and Map (AIGAM) is a single trip planner platform that integrates navigation functionalities with itinerary planning, allowing users to create, manage, and follow their travel plans more efficiently. This testing document recommends strategies for integration and validation testing across the major subsystems of AIGAM: the backend microservices, the middleware orchestration layer, and the frontend user interface. Functionally, the scope includes testing navigation behavior, itinerary generation and management, and notification behavior triggered by itinerary updates. The test plan also covers how external dependencies such as the AI model, mapping and weather providers, and the push notification service are integrated and exercised through the backend and middleware builds.

In addition to core functionality, this document addresses non-functional testing for AIGAM, focusing on latency, concurrency, resilience, and security as defined in the functional and non-functional requirements from the AIGAM project proposal.
